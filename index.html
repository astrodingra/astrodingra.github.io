About

Hey, I'm Jashanpreet, your gateway to the stars. Hailing from India, I'm fueled by an insatiable curiosity for the universe. I immerse myself in the enigmatic realms of astronomy, deciphering the language of celestial data and unearthing the mysteries of Active Galactic Nuclei (AGNs).   As the president of the Dingrastro Club., I orchestrate cosmic spectacles, igniting minds with the wonders of the cosmos.   I've clinched accolades— a third-place triumph at the 2021 International Astronomy and Astrophysics Competition, a runner-up in the South Down Astrophotography Contest 2021, and honored as an NSEA 2023 scholar .
I am soon diving into a college for my BSC program in physics, another step toward my goal. However I have done many astronomical analysis and research which you check at research section.

My top research and analysis include the understanding of AGN galaxies's star formation rate correlation with their mass and metallicity, and studing the redshift of type Ia supernovae. Both research data are collected from SDSS using Casjob data query and SDSS query using Python . Along with analysis i also wrote few articles which you can check on articles section. Besides astronomy and outreach, I’m an enthusiast for sketching portraits and photography.
